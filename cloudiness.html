Cloudiness

A point of interest for the Latitude vs. Cloudiness plot is that there are clear groups of observations on the factors of 20% lines. However, there seems to be an exception at 60% and 80% where this group is actually on the 75% line. I assume that "cloudiness" reports are typically rounded to nice even values which is why we observe these groupings. Why many reports use the 75% rounded value instead of 60% or 80% whereas the other rounded reports are all factors of 20% is a bit odd. Maybe I am making the wrong assumption and the measure of cloudiness is very scientific and it just so happens that we rarely see 60% or 80% recordings but I am skeptical of this.
